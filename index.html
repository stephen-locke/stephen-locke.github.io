About Me

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Economics at Western Kentucky University in Bowling Green, Kentucky. I earned my B.A. in Economics from WKU in 2008 and my Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Kentucky in 2013. Before joining the WKU faculty, I served as a postdoctoral researcher in the Institute of the Environment and Sustainability at the University of California, Los Angeles.
